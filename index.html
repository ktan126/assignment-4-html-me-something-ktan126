    To Love

    To Love, starring Lin Gengxin and Gai Yuexi, is a story about Yan Jin, a former anti-narcotics cop who goes undercover to clear his name over the murder of his best friend. While investigating the drug trafficking ring involved of his best friend's murder, he becomes close to Ji Xiao Ou, a coffee shop owner, who then helps him clear his name and solve the murder of one of her employees.

    Arsenal Military Academy

    Starring Xu Kai and Bai Lu, Arsenal Military Academy is set during the Republican era of China when the Japanese Imperial Army tried to occupy China. Xie Xiang disguises herself as her deceased brother in order to join the military academy. There, becomes classmates with Gu Yanzhen and Shen Jun Shan, who then becomes her comrade to fight against the Japanese while keeping her identity a secret.

    The Untamed

    Wei Wuxian, played by Xiao Zhan, has died during the battles in the Nightless City. 16 years later, he is resurrected through a self-sacrificing ritual and has to conceal himself with a mask. He is reunited with his old friend and soulmate, Lan Wangji, played by Wang Yibo, and together they solve the mysteries of the present and figure out the truth that brought him to his death 16 years ago.

    Korean Dramas

    Mr. Sunshine

    A young boy, Choi Yu-Jin born into a house servant's family was forced to flee after he saw his parents killed by his masters. He escaped to the US during the 1871 Shinmiyangyo and since then swore to become an American. Many years later, he is now the captain of the US Marine Corps and returns to Korea for a mission. In the US, he meets a noblewoman, Go Ae-Shin, who is a member of the Righteous Army. Starring Lee Byung-Hun and Kim Tae-Ri, Mr. Sunshine tells the story of Joseon before the Japanese annexation, the Righteous Army who fought for the independence of Korea, and the love story between an American soldier and a Joseon aristrocat.

    Reply 1988

    Reply 1988 tell the stories of five families in the Ssangmundong neighborhood of Seoul in 1988.
